Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

At Prize24 ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Prize24 mobile application and website (the "Service").

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our Service:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: We track your interactions with the App, including which Vendors you visit, the QR codes you scan ("Streaks"), rewards you redeem, and the dates/times of your visits.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we may collect precise or approximate location data to show you nearby participating Vendors and relevant offers in your "District."
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the Service, including the hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To maintain your "Streaks" and loyalty progress.
  • To enable Vendors to identify you as a loyal customer.
  • To provide customer support and troubleshoot issues.
  • To send you transactional emails (e.g., password resets) and promotional notifications (if opted in) regarding local offers.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve the App's functionality.

3 Sharing Information with Vendors

Crucial Note Prize24 is a connection platform. When you engage with a specific business, data sharing occurs.

When you scan a QR code at a participating Vendor or redeem a reward, we share your profile information (Name, Profile Picture, Visit History with that specific Vendor) with that Vendor.

This allows the Vendor to recognize you, track your loyalty status, and offer personalized service. We do not share your visit history from Vendor A with Vendor B, unless they are part of a specific cross-promotional campaign you have engaged with.

4 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your data with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Cloud hosting providers (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Supabase).
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) for subscription handling.
  • Email delivery services.

5 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure servers) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6 Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location (e.g., California), you may have specific rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Correction: You can view and edit your profile data within the App settings.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt-out of push notifications or marketing emails at any time via your device settings or the unsubscribe link in emails.

7 Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
